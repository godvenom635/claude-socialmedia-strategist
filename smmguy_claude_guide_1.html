5 minutes. 5 clicks. Done.

01

⚙️
02

Click into Connectors

🔌
03

Hit "Browse Connectors"

Opens up the full directory of available connectors. Search bar at the top.

🔎

Part 3 — The Prompts

Copy. Paste. Watch it work.

Once Windsor AI is connected, paste any of these prompts into Claude. Each one builds on the last.

Prompt 01 Start here

Run a full performance audit

Use Windsor AI. Analyse my Instagram @yourhandle. Identify my top 5 viral posts and bottom 5 flops in the last 6 months. Find the exact patterns — hooks, format, captions, timing — that separate them.

What you get: A clear list of what's working vs killing your reach, with the actual data behind it.

Prompt 02

Map your real audience

Pull my audience demographics from Windsor — age, gender, country, top cities. Build a 1-paragraph persona of who is actually watching me, and tell me the 3 content angles this person responds to most.

What you get: A clear persona, not a guess. Your content angles for the next 30 days come from this.

Prompt 03 ⭐ The big one

Build a 30-day content calendar

Based on my top-performing patterns and audience persona, build me a 30-day content calendar. 20 posts across 4 weeks. For each, give me: format (Reel / Carousel / Static), the exact hook, the core value proposition, and a one-word Comment-CTA keyword.

What you get: A done-for-you calendar built around what actually works for YOUR account, not generic advice.

Prompt 04

Find your repost candidates

Look through my last 6 months. Find posts with high average watch time (over 9 seconds) but low total views. These are content the algorithm under-served. Suggest a new hook and cover for each one so I can repost.

What you get: Free content. Your old work, repackaged for a second chance at reach.

Prompt 05

Audit your hooks

Group my last 50 captions by hook type — outcome hooks, numbered lists, contrarian takes, philosophical, profile-frame, etc. Tell me which hook types are pulling above and below my median views. Give me 10 better hooks I should be using.

What you get: Which hook formulas to double down on and which to delete from your repertoire.

Prompt 06

Build your DM keyword swipe file

From my content calendar, list every "Comment KEYWORD" CTA and what deliverable each one should send. Draft me the auto-DM message for each keyword so I can pre-load them before posting.

What you get: Auto-DM responses ready BEFORE the viral hits, not scrambling after.

Part 4 — Pro Tips

Five things I learned the hard way.

Read these before you start. They'll save you a week of wasted prompting.

!

Always tell Claude to use Windsor AI

If you don't mention Windsor in the prompt, Claude won't pull real data — it'll guess. Always start with "Use Windsor AI…"

2

Specify the time range

"Last 6 months" gives more data; "last 30 days" gives recent trends. Pick based on what you're asking.

3

Ask for the WHY, not just the WHAT

Don't just ask "what worked?" — ask "what worked and WHY?" The why is where your next viral lives.

4

Save rate beats like rate

When you ask Claude to rank your content, tell it to weight save rate and share rate over likes. That's what Instagram actually pushes.

5

Re-run the audit every 30 days

Your audience evolves. What worked in March may not work in May. Re-run the full audit monthly and update your calendar.
